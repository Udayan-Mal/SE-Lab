How to use these diagrams in Draw.io

  1. Click on any diagram above to view it
  2. Click the "Copy Code" button to copy the Mermaid code
  3. Open Draw.io and create a new diagram
  4. Click on "+ More Shapes" at the bottom left
  5. Enable the "Mermaid" option and click "Apply"
  6. Drag the Mermaid shape onto your canvas
  7. Paste the copied code into the Mermaid shape
  8. Click "Insert" to render the diagram
